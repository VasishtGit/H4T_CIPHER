Find the equation of a line passing through (2,3) with slope 4.

Detailed Explanation

Step by Step

Given Information

We are given one point on the line: (2,3) and slope m = 4.

Apply Point-Slope Form

Use the equation:

y - y₁ = m(x - x₁)

Substitute values:

y - 3 = 4(x - 2)

Simplify

Expand and rearrange:

y - 3 = 4x - 8

y = 4x - 5

Final Answer

The required line equation is y = 4x - 5.
